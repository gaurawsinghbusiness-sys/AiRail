WHAT IS AI RAILWAY SIM?

AI Railway Sim is an autonomous infrastructure simulation where AI agents plan and build a futuristic railway network in real-time.

COMMANDER (Gemini)

Directs the strategic expansion. Decides where the network should go next based on current topology.

WORKER (Groq)

Executes the commander's orders. Uses Llama 3 to calculate coordinates and build new stations and tracks instantly.

Watch the map expand as the agents work together to colonize the digital canvas.
